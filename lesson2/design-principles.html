Design Principles

PARC: Contrast

www.deseret.com

The choice for the color Yellow in the header and footer contrast very well with the black color of the writing and alo the style for the font with bold and regular and also the Script like styler they used for the logo give a very subtle contrast. It brings more attention to the logo. And the bold, brings attention to the importancy of the Headlines.

PARC: Alignment

www.cnn.com

The alignment choice for the headline, even as Centered works very well bringing the right tension to the news in spotlight. We should try to avoid the centered alignment because it can become boring and untractive very easily, but here is a very good example of it, if you use it correctly it can bring a nice touch to the design and also creates a contrast with the rest of the page that is left Aligned

White Space and Clean Design

www.deseret.com

The white space on the page is a place for the eyes to rest, when the page has to many elements it gives an impression of crowded space and instead of resting it brings stress. Here in this page we can see that every news has its own place and they are not competing for attention even the pictures are very well sized and its position helps to keep the design organized
