Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide people with the weather update, 24hs, 7 days a week, at Cities of:
- Fish Haven
- Preston
- Soda Springs
We want to provide the current weather and help all Citizens and also all visitors to be prepared for their day activities and enjoy even more the outdoors and our beautiful place. We want this to be a web site with easy access to all the information, with easy understanding and accessible from any device you use.
